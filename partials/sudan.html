• 2006 May

  • Inflation starts ...

    Year-on-year inflation exceeds 1,000%. New banknotes, with three noughts deleted from their values, are introduced in August.
  • 2007 May

  • Power cuts and social unrest ...

    Warnings of power cuts for up to 20 hours a day while electricity is diverted towards agriculture.
  • 10 Feb. 2014

  • Health Emergency ...

    Zimbabwe declares national emergency over a cholera epidemic and the collapse of its health care system.
  • 2009 January

  • Government, finally takes charge ...

    Government allows use of foreign currencies to try stem hyperinflation.
  • 2009 March

  • Light at the end of the tunnel ...

    Retail prices fall for the first time after years of hyperinflation.
